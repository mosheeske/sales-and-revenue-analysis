# 📊 Sales and Revenue Analysis

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes sales data to identify revenue patterns, customer behavior, and key performance metrics. The analysis focuses on understanding how different factors influence sales performance and business outcomes.

## 🎯 Objective
To evaluate sales performance and extract actionable insights that support business decisions related to revenue growth and customer behavior.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Python
- pandas
- Data Cleaning
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Aggregation and metrics calculation

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset includes transactional sales data such as:
- Customer information
- Purchase records
- Revenue values
- Product-related data

## 🔍 Key Steps
- Cleaned and prepared raw sales data
- Handled missing values and corrected inconsistencies
- Aggregated sales metrics across different dimensions
- Analyzed revenue distribution and trends
- Identified key drivers of sales performance
- Compared customer behavior across segments

## 📈 Key Findings
- Revenue is concentrated among a subset of customers
- Certain patterns indicate opportunities for increasing sales
- Customer behavior varies across segments
- Sales trends reveal potential optimization areas

## 💡 Business Impact
This analysis can help companies:
- Improve revenue strategies
- Identify high-value customers
- Optimize sales processes
- Support data-driven commercial decisions
- Enhance targeting and personalization
